Health Sandwich

Description

Pretty much an ordinary sandwich, but HEALTHIFIED. Taking care of what you eat feels good!

I try to get as many vitamins and nutrients as I can, as consistently as I can. This sandwich makes it easy!

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Your choice of protein. I personally used sliced chicken.
  • Pre-chopped garlic
  • Cheese
  • A bit of mayo
  • Ponzu sauce! Dijon mustard works as well.
  • Kale!
  • Cilantro!

Steps

  1. Spread your mayo on your bread slices
  2. Add a slice of cheese
  3. Add your protein source on top
  4. Optional: toast or microwave for extra yumminess!
  5. Add your chopped garlic
  6. Add your kale and cilantro
  7. Add mustard or ponzu sauce on top of the leafy greens for flavor
  8. Eat your creation and nourish your cells!
