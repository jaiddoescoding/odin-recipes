Berry Jam

Description

Homemade berry jam! You can use any berry you would like. Choose your favorite!

A sweet, sticky, delicious treat packed with vitamins and minerals! Yay fruit!

Ingredients

  • Berries of your choice
  • Lemon juice
  • Your choice of sweetener: for example, honey or sugar
  • Optional: vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Throw berries into pot
  2. Turn on heat on stove, set to medium/low
  3. Mash up the berries in the pot! Turn them into mush!
  4. Stir with a spoon. Turn up heat. Wait for mashed berries to boil
  5. Add some squirts of lemon juice and stir
  6. Add some sugar or honey. Use your eyes to determine the amount
  7. Let boil and occassionally stir. Add in optional vanilla extract too, if you like vanilla. Best with blueberries and cherries in my opinion!
