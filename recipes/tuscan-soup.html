Tuscan Soup

Description

A warm and creamy delicacy. Infused with Italian spices and cheesey cream. Includes onion, garlic, sausage, potatoes, kale, heavy cream, and cheese.

It is simple to make and you can do it all in one pot!

Ingredients

  • heavy whipping cream
  • one whole onion
  • whole garlic bulb
  • kale
  • pre-cooked sausage
  • chicken broth
  • red potatoes
  • parmesan cheese
  • a whole lot of spices

Steps

  1. Chop onion and garlic, add to heated pot
  2. Chop sausage and add to pot
  3. Add some olive oil, rosemary, thyme, basil, and oregeno
  4. Add chicken broth to pot and bring to a boil
  5. Chop up potatoes and add to pot
  6. Once potatoes are soft, turn the heat off. Add heavy cream and kale.
  7. Stir everything together! Let cool
  8. Get a bowl and add parmesan cheese! YUMMY
